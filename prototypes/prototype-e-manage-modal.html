Manage Categories — Modal Dialog

Opens a focused two-pane modal from a button in the existing config toolbar. Categories on the left, subcategories of the selected primary on the right. Edits are staged and committed atomically with Save, or discarded with Cancel.

  • Click Manage categories in the toolbar to open the modal.
  • Click a primary on the left to view/edit its subcategories on the right.
  • Click the emoji to open the picker. Double-click a name to rename inline.
  • Drag the ≡ handle to reorder. Hover a row for the delete (×) button.
  • Add via the dashed button at the bottom of each pane.

Strengths

  • Familiar admin pattern; high focus.
  • Atomic save = easy undo via Cancel.
  • Two-pane scales to many subs without scroll-jumping.

Weaknesses

  • Hides the underlying transactions table while open.
  • Extra click to enter "edit mode".
  • Save/Cancel adds friction for tiny edits.

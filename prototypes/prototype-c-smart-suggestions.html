Prototype C

Pill Tags with Searchable Combobox

  • Categories displayed as color-coded pill tags for quick visual scanning
  • Click any pill to open a searchable category picker
  • After selecting, choose to update all matching transactions or just one
  • Toast notification confirms the change with an undo option
  • "+ Opprett ny kategori" option at the bottom of the dropdown

Pros vs others

  • Searchable picker scales with many categories (A, B, D use plain dropdowns)
  • Explicit undo via toast notification (unique)
  • Choose “update all” vs “just this one” — precise scope control
  • Create new category inline without leaving the table
  • Color-coded pills are the most visually scannable

Cons vs others

  • Extra confirmation step adds clicks (A applies immediately)
  • No panel view for bulk editing many merchants at once (B has one)
  • No sort-based grouping of uncategorized rows (D offers this)
  • No rule persistence across sessions (D persists rules in localStorage)
